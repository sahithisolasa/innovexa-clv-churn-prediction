# Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) & Churn Prediction

## 📌 Overview

This project is an end-to-end Data Science case study developed for the Innovexa Catalyst Data Science Challenge. The objective is to predict Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) and identify customers who are likely to churn using customer demographics, transaction history, engagement metrics, and support interactions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Predict Customer Lifetime Value (Regression)
- Predict Customer Churn (Classification)
- Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Generate Business Insights and Recommendations

## 📊 Models

### CLV Prediction

- Random Forest Regressor
- MAE: 41.64
- RMSE: 52.07
- R² Score: 94.90%

### Churn Prediction

- Random Forest Classifier
- Accuracy: 89.40%
- Precision: 72.73%
- Recall: 47.68%
- F1 Score: 57.60%
- ROC-AUC: 72.25%

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Monetary value is the strongest predictor of Customer Lifetime Value.
- Refund count is the most influential factor for customer churn.
- Premium customers generally generate higher lifetime value.
- Customer retention strategies should focus on refund reduction and engagement improvement.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase customer spending through personalized offers.
- Promote premium subscription plans.
- Identify high-risk customers early.
- Reduce refund rates through proactive customer support.
